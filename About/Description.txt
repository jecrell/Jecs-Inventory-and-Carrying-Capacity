[h1]A simple mod to boost carrying capacity based on player settings.[/h1]

Tired of not having enough inventory capacity or characters unable to carry everything you need?
This mod will allow you to modify the carrying capacity and inventory capacity of pawns in RimWorld.

Settings are available in the Mod Settings menu under Jec's Inventory and Carrying Capacity.
Multipliers in the settings menu will allow players to increase inventory or carrying capacity independently up to 10x normal.
Body Size related modifiers can also be disabled from the carrying capacity / inventory capacity calculations.
